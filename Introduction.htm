WHAT IS PLAGIARISM?

Plagiarism is Presenting work or ideas from another source

as your own, with or without the consent of the original author, by

incorporating it into your work without full acknowledgement. In short,

plagiarism is stealing someones work and pretending to be the author

of that work.
